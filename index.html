Why Topmarks fonts?

The Topmarks fonts are being developed in response to teachers and educators who find a lack of free and open source typefaces suitable for use in infant learning. The project aims to widen the choice of suitable typefaces that can be used freely and easily in everyday teaching.

About the design

The Topmarks fonts are based on the free software font family 'Muli', a sans-serif typeface that has proved popular with children, teachers and even toy makers, but the forms of Muli are not quite right for infants.

Free and open

The Topmarks fonts are released under the Open Font License (OFL). This means that the fonts are free to be used by as many children and their teachers as need to without restriction.
