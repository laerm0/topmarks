About the design of Topmarks Fonts

The design of Topmarks fonts is based on the Free Software font 'Muli'.

Muli has already proved a useful font in classrooms, as it is a simple and very legible design, so it was a good starting point for Topmarks. The underlying basic structure of Topmarks and Muli are the same, so they also make good companions when used together.

Topmarks is being developed in various weights and versions to suit the different needs found in children's learning.

Roadmap & Funding

The Topmarks Fonts have been steadily under development for nearly 12 months. It is now planned to raise development funding via indiegogo so that the fonts can be developed further, and completed in a more concentrated effort.
