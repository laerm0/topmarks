Free and open

The Topmarks fonts are released under the Open Font License. This license allows the fonts to be used freely, by unlimited numbers of users.

The Topmarks fonts can be used for printing, as webfonts, for setting digital documents, and embedding in other software, used in commercial or non-commercial work, pretty much anything. Also, being Free Software, the Topmarks fonts can be shared, given away, or even altered.

About the design

The Topmarks fonts are based on the free software font family 'Muli', a typeface that has already proven popular with children and teachers.

The forms of Muli are straightforward and easy to read, and extra features have been introduced with Topmarks to help children forming their own handwriting, for example, the extension of the ends of strokes to encourage a connection between the printed word and handwriting.
