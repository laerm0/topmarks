About the design

The Topmarks fonts are based on the free software font family 'Muli', a sans-serif typeface that has already proven popular with children and teachers. The forms of Muli are straightforward and easy to read, but lack other type features that can help young children starting to form their own handwriting. The Topmarks design takes the basic Muli letterforms but also incorporates forms and functions that are helpfull to young.
