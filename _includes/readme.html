Free and open

The Topmarks fonts are being created in response to teachers and educators who find a lack of free and open source typefaces suitable for use in infant learning.

The Topmarks fonts are released under the Open Font License. This license allows the fonts to be used freely, by an unlimited numbers of users. The Topmarks fonts can be used for printing, for setting digital documents, used as webfonts, embedded in documents or in other software. Also, being Free Software, the Topmarks fonts can be shared, given away or even altered.

About the design

The Topmarks fonts are based on the free software font family 'Muli', a sans-serif typeface that has already proven popular with children and teachers. The forms of Muli are straightforward and easy to read, but lack other type features that can help young children starting to form their own handwriting. The Topmarks design takes the basic Muli letterforms but also incorporates forms and functions that are helpfull to young.
