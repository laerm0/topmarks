Free and open

The Topmarks fonts are being created in response to teachers and educators who find a lack of Free Software typefaces suitable for use in infant learning.

The Topmarks fonts are released under the Open Font License. This license allows the fonts to be used freely, by unlimited numbers of users.

The Topmarks fonts can be used for printing, as webfonts, for setting digital documents, and embedding in other software, commercial or non-commercial work, pretty much anything in fact. Also, being Free Software, the Topmarks fonts can be shared, given away, or even altered.

About the design

The Topmarks fonts are based on the free software font family 'Muli', a typeface that has already proven popular with children and teachers.

The design of Topmarks uses the basic Muli letterforms but also incorporates aspects that are helpfull to young learners.

The forms of Muli are straightforward and easy to read, plus extra features have been introduced, for example the extension of the ends of strokes that encourage a connection between the printed word and handwriting to help children form their own handwriting.
