Topmarks

The Topmarks fonts are being created in response to teachers and educators who find a lack of Free Software typefaces suitable for use in infant learning.

The aim of developing the fonts is to widen the choice of suitable typefaces that can be used freely and easily in everyday teaching.

Free and open

The Topmarks fonts are released under the Open Font License, allowing the fonts to be used freely, by unlimited numbers of users.

The fonts can be used for printing, as webfonts, for setting digital documents, embedding in other software, used in commercial or non-commercial work, and being Free Software, the Topmarks fonts can be shared, given away, or even altered.
